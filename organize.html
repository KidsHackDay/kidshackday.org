How does it work?

Kids Hack Day is a global open source brand, free of charge for anyone to use. By creating an entry in our open wiki you can get a license for organizing 1 KHD event in your city. After you’ve held your event you can then renew this license or pass it on to someone else. The only thing we demand in return is that your event is well documented and free of charge for the kids. This means you will have to work with local sponsors to fund your event. This is a setup we’ve created to ensure we also reach kids in more underprivileged areas and have maximum social impact.
