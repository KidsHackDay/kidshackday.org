HACKS! What happens at a KHD

What is a hack? A hack is a quick solution to a problem – maybe not the most elegant solution, but often the cleverest.

Whether you’re into computers, arts, music, science the combined personal interests of you and your team will of course affect what hacks you chose to do at your event. What happens at a Kids Hack Day is not constrained to specific topics and can really incorporate anything. While the focus is on technology hacking is really all about a mindset. All Kids Hack Day events are unified through this hacker mindset. A shared belief that if we can invoke the little hacker inside all of us, the world would be a much better place.

To allow kids to document what they are making during a Kid's Hack Day Event we have partnered up with Creatubbles, a safe, creative and global online community for kids. Below is a selection of projects made by kids and shared on Creatubbles. If you want to see all projects please visit our Creatubbles gallery
