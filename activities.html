What happens at a KHD?

Whether you’re into computers, arts, music, science the combined personal interests of you and your team will of course affect the theme of your event. What happens at a Kids Hack Day is not constrained to specific topics and can really incorporate anything (also see “what happens at a khd event”). While the focus is on technology hacking is really all about a mindset. All Kids Hack Day events are unified through this hacker mindset. A shared belief that if we can invoke the little hacker inside all of us, the world would be a much better place.
