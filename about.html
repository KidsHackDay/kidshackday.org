Who we are

What is KHD?

Kid's Hack Day is a global series of 1-day free-of-charge creative tech events for kids. At a KHD event kids are given tools to express their creativity, be curious and express empathy towards each other and the world around them. Ultimately we exist to empower kids to make the world a better place. We do this by giving them tools to reclaim what is increasingly being robbed them but what has always been theirs. The future.

Kids Hack Day Manifesto

Why hacking?

We believe in the hacker-mindset, which has nothing to do with computers really. We define this mindset as understanding that the world is not static and something you have the power to interact with. The objects around you have all been created by someone who had one particular intention with its use, yes. But the objects around you can also be picked apart, studied, changed, modified and improved to fit your own needs. A fork is for eating because someone told you it is. But a fork can also be an amazing musical instrument, or an interface to a computer. Whatever you imagine, can happen. This mindset is inherit in kids but are too often squashed as they go through traditional education. In this sense, it is as important for us adults to reconnect with this mindset as it is for kids to stay in it.

Every Kids Hack Day event is different.

Because every Kids Hack Day organizer works under radically different conditions each event will also have radically different levels of ambition. Depending on the amount of resources and skills you have access to in your team your event might be super small with only a handful of kids in a tiny local makerspace or school or it might be a massive public event with hundreds of kids attending. We encourage this diversity and organizing a small and humble event is not something that should hold you back, neither should doing a really big one.

Every organizer is different.

Whether you’re into computers, arts, music, science the combined personal interests of you and your team will of course affect the theme of your event. What happens at a Kids Hack Day is not constrained to specific topics and can really incorporate anything (also see “what happens at a khd event” ). While the focus is on technology hacking is really all about a mindset. All Kids Hack Day events are unified through this hacker mindset. A shared belief that if we can invoke the little hacker inside all of us, the world would be a much better place.

Every kid is different.

Kids Hack Day events are all about resourceful use of material and technology to make young kids feel empowered by exposing them to a new way of relating to the world, one where they feel that they are in control over their own future. Attaining this feeling might be done with very different tools and methods because every kid is different and kids are interested in different things. As an organizer it is your job and privilege to encourage all kids to follow their own path, whatever that might be. Inspiring a kid to find their path is probably the most rewarding and inspiring thing you can do.

Our Story

Inspired by the global hackerspace movement and the lack of technology-related play and creativity in the classroom, Kids Hack Day is an idea that sprung out of a first series of workshops run by lead instigator and former TEDxStockholm organizer Carl Bärstad under the name Hackerspace In A Box as an attempt to strengthen the collaboration between schools and local hackerspaces in the region.
